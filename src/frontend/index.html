Collatz Conjecture

Let's take a positive integer number and perform the following actions:
If n1 is even then n2 = 1/2 * n1; if n1 is odd then n2 = 3*n1 + 1.
Continue doing it as much as needed.
Collatz conjecture claims that by doing these two operations you will eventually come to 1.
The resulting sequence from initial number to 1 is called Collatz path.
